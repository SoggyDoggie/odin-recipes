Lemon Rosmary Salmon

Description:

This delicious rosmary and lemon salmon is the perfect blend of tangy citrus and sharp rosemary, making this a luxurious dish the whole family can enjoy.

  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • 4 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 2 salmon fillets, bones and skin removed
  • coarse salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil as needed
